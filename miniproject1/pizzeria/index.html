FAQ

Ordering from us is simple! Just browse our menu, customize your pizza, add it to your cart, and proceed to checkout for fast delivery right to your door.

Our pizzas are delivered in approximately 30 minutes, ensuring you enjoy your meal while it's fresh and hot

Yes, we offer special catering options for large parties, allowing you to enjoy our delicious pizzas with your guests!

how much protein it contains?
